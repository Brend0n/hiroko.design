FarmUp!

A Smart Pest Control Management App for Farmers

Overview
FarmUP! is an Android app that helps farmers manage pest control schedules and track pesticide use for each crop.
The Problem
Japan’s strict pesticide regulations frequently change, but most farmers still rely on handwritten notes, increasing the risk of errors that could impact food safety. With their already heavy workload, they need an easier way to stay compliant. >
The Solution
FarmUP! streamlines farm management with:
  • Pest Control Management – Checks pesticide compliance, tracks usage, and calculates optimal amounts.
  • Field & Plantation Management – Organizes crops efficiently.
  • Schedule Management – Keeps track of application schedules.
A regularly updated database ensures farmers always have the latest regulatory information.
Design Approach
I focused on simplicity with four main functions at the top: Schedule, Pest Control, Farm, and Plantation Management. A color-coding system helps farmers track crops visually. Given limited resources, I designed a clean layout to minimize development effort while maximizing usability.
