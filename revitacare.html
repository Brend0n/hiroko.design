A Holistic Health Companion for Employees

Our client wanted a better way to communicate health and wellness initiatives between the HR department and employees. The challenge? HR had little to no visibility into employees' health needs, making it difficult to offer the right wellness programs. However, employee well-being directly impacts healthcare costs, productivity, and turnover rates. Together, we created a holistic health companion—a unique solution that supports employees' daily health while providing anonymous health insights to HR. This allows for better wellness program planning and improved overall well-being in the workplace.
As the sole designer on this project, I took on both product design and strategic collaboration, working closely with our clients to ensure their vision was effectively translated into the final product.

A Truly Holistic Health Experience

There are countless digital health solutions available—workout apps, meditation guides, sleep trackers, nutrition planners—but they often operate in isolation even in reality, all aspects of health are deeply interconnected. Also each solution doesn't address exact individual needs. To bridge this gap, we defined six essential health pillars and leveraged AI-powered personalization. Our LLM-based algorithm tailors content based on the user’s goals, health conditions, and preferences, providing a fully customized experience for each employee.

Boosting Engagement Through Gamification

To make wellness engaging, we designed a gamified experience where users set daily health goals. When they achieve them, they can grow flowers and plant them in their personal garden. Each health pillar corresponds to a unique garden element, so every user’s garden becomes a reflection of their wellness journey. This fun and rewarding approach keeps employees motivated to stay on track with their health.

Bridging the Gap Between
HR & Employees

We also created a seamless communication channel between HR and employees. HR can share wellness events, exclusive offers, and resources, while employees can easily access, book, and redeem them through the app. Additionally, employees can provide feedback on wellness programs, ensuring HR gets real-time insights into what’s working and what needs improvement. This two-way interaction fosters better visibility and stronger workplace well-being.
