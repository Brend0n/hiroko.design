bunbogu.log

Share your love to stationeries with others

Bunbogu.log is a web-based community for stationery lovers, created as a passion project by my friend, a web engineer. A huge fan of stationery, she wanted to build a platform where users could share reviews and discover new stationery products.
The platform’s main features include:
  • Adding new stationery products and writing reviews
  • Searching for stationery by rating and personal preferences
  • Writing reviews for existing products
  • Sharing favorite stationery finds with others
I designed the user experience with a smartphone-first approach, as we envisioned most users accessing the platform on mobile devices. Since this was a project among friends, I also took on logo design and graphic design responsibilities.
