BConnect

A Development Tool for Rapid SDK Generation

BConnect is a development tool that generates SDKs for mobile apps, web, firmware, and backend systems based on user-provided specifications. These SDKs cover essential features like account handling and connectivity, significantly reducing development time.
For the mobile SDKs, I designed a simple and intuitive design system. To ensure usability even for non-designers, I introduced a theme concept with a structured color palette and predefined values. This approach allows easy customization of the design and overall look while maintaining a consistent and user-friendly experience.
