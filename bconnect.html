Creating design system for a SDK generator

Project: BConnect Design System
Device: iOS, Android app
Role: Product Design
BConnect is a development tool that generates SDKs for mobile apps, web, firmware, and backend systems based on user-provided specifications. These SDKs are used across both internal and external projects within the Baracoda group. For the mobile SDKs, I created a simple yet flexible design system to ensure seamless integration into different projects. To achieve this, I introduced a theme concept with a structured color palette and predefined values. This approach makes it easy to customize the design and overall look while maintaining a consistent, user-friendly experience. The design system has been adopted in most Baracoda projects I contributed to, including BMirror, BHeart, and various client projects. As both a designer and a user of the product, I continuously iterate on the system to improve its usability.
