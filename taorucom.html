Taoru.com

EC site renuwal

I was given the opportunity to renew the taoru.com website, originally created in the 1980s.
The main goal of the renewal was to transform the outdated site into a modern, responsive website to improve accessibility and attract more frequent customers. The original website’s main feature, the “Order Original Towel” flow, was designed primarily for professional use and was too complex for general users—despite them being the target audience. To address this, I streamlined the ordering process by eliminating unnecessary steps and simplifying it into two clear ordering methods.
Additionally, I optimized the site structure for SEO, which significantly improved its visibility. As a result, taoru.com now appears on the first page of organic search results for the keyword "泉州タオル オリジナル作成".
Beyond the website, I also designed various printed materials for taoru.com.
