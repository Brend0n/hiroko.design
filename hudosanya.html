Hudosanya

A rental appartmant portal for lifestyle

Like many other developed cities around the world, it is becoming increasingly difficult for young people in Tokyo to find a partner or opportunities to meet new people.
While there are many rental apartment portal sites, none focus on lifestyle after moving in. Hudosanya allows users to search for apartments based on the character of the city. For example, if you often dine out late, you might choose a neighborhood with many late-night restaurants. If you want to socialize with people of a similar age, you could select a city popular among your generation.
Additionally, Hudosanya offers a unique “Find an Apartment by Fortune Telling” feature, which suggests the best apartment for you based on your horoscope and a Lenormand card reading—both supervised by professional fortune tellers. This feature may appeal to young women interested in exploring their lifestyle from a different perspective.
