Like any other developed city all over the world, it is becoming harder and harder for young people to find a partner or opportunity to meet new people in Tokyo.
There are lots of rental apartment portal sites but none of them suggest the life style after living in the apartment. Hudosanya offers you to find apartments by the feature of the city ( if you often go for dinner late at night, you may choose “the city with lots of restaurants open till late”, if you want to hang out with same generation people, you may choose “the city popular for your age”, etc). Also there is a function “find apartment by fortune telling”, hudosanya suggest you the best apartment according to the result of your fortune. The fortune is derived by horoscopes and Lenormand card, both supervised by professional fortune tellers. It may attract young woman who want to find their lifestyle by different aspect.
The main target is young woman (age 20th 30th) who has stable job and salary, wants to enjoy after work, looking for new opportunity to meet people.
