CouCoudeFrance

French discovery website

Having lived in France, I am a huge fan of the country—especially its food and wine! As a passion project, I created Coucou de France (which means "Hello from France"), a platform that introduces authentic French culture, cuisine, and travel destinations to a Japanese audience.
While there are countless websites about French culture and travel, most Japanese sources focus only on Paris and high-end, three-star restaurants. However, through my own experiences, I have discovered France’s beautiful countryside, incredible regional dishes, local wines, cheeses, and more.
That’s why I decided to create a platform dedicated to sharing France’s rich local culture with Japan. All the illustrations and photos on the website are my own original work.
