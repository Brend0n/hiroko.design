Since I have experienced living in France, I’m huge fun of France (especially food and wine!). Here is my hobby project calls Coucou de France (means Hello from France), which introduces French real local culture, food, travel destination to Japan. Of course there are thousand of website talking about French culture and traveling there, but the information we can find in Japanese websites is only about Paris and three stars restaurants. I have discovered beautiful country sides, fantastic local meals from different regions, wine, cheese, etc.  So I decided to make platform of French local information to Japan. The illustrations and pictures in this website are all drew and shot by me.
