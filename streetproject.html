Volunteer: Street Project

When I was living in Sydney, Australia, I worked at an organic bakery that also ran a community volunteer project. The project collected organic waste from nearby shops, turned it into compost, and used it to grow herbs and vegetables in flower pots along the street. I helped with the project and created hand-drawn signs for the pots. It was a truly memorable experience. I learned how kindness and cooperation can connect people, no matter who they are or where they come from. I loved drawing those beautiful flowers, vegetables, and herbs—and even more, I loved seeing people stop to look at the signs, learn about the project, and decide to join in. It was wonderful to witness ✨
