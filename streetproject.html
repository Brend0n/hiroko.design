Street Project

Cultivating Community Through Green Spaces

Street Project was a community-driven volunteer initiative based on Macpherson Street in Sydney, Australia—a charming local market street lined with restaurants and shops. While studying in Sydney, I worked at a bakery on this very street, which made my involvement in the project even more meaningful.
The project aimed to grow organic vegetables, herbs, and flowers in street-side pots using compost made from organic waste collected from local shops. I contributed not only by helping with the gardening but also by creating illustrations and graphic designs for signage and novelty items to raise small funds for project maintenance.
From touching the rich organic soil to watering thriving plants, harvesting fresh produce, and collaborating with a diverse community, every moment was deeply rewarding. Observing and sketching nature’s beauty brought me a profound sense of serenity. I am truly grateful for the opportunity to have been part of this heartwarming project!
